About Us

The Viredear Art Gallery is a curated art portal with only a few curators. We guarantee authenticity, transparency, and price to all our artists. With us, you may buy paintings online.


Our History

Started in 2020, VIRIDEAR is quickly establishing itself as a artwork icon par excellence in Mumbai. With its unique way of selling artistic works, this experience can be found nowhere else. Featuring four of the best three-star artists in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

VIRIDEAR is a tech-enabled online art gallery and a market disruptor in the art space. For the longest time, paintings and artworks are confined mostly to the four walls of traditional art galleries. Viridear has taken the digital-first approach and was set up to democratize art. We aim to provide art buyers an opportunity to acquire art at attractive price points along with making the buying process easy. It offers a platform for artists to showcase their work and reach out to a wider audience and not be restricted to their local markets or cities.


Our Motive

To be a platform that introduces a diverse range of artworks from local artists to the worldwide market. To provide a forum where artists may compete fairly with other international artists. To create an online art store and exhibitions to provide additional opportunity for artists to develop, and customers to explore especially now during the pandemic.

  • Bring Global Art to the doorstep of art lovers at attractive price.
  • Encourage & Nurture established and emerging talent.
  • Simplify the Art buying process.
